We get one question more than any other: *how do I join as a tipster?*
Here's the short version.

## The process

1. Create a free TipHub account on the [register page](/register).
2. From your profile, fill out the [tipster application form](/tipster/apply).
   It takes about ten minutes — we ask for a sample of three picks with
   reasoning, your specialty league or market, and a short bio.
3. An admin reviews every application by hand. We aim to respond within
   24 hours and you'll get an email either way.
4. If approved, your account is promoted to tipster role and you can
   start posting picks immediately from `/tipster/dashboard`.

That's the whole flow. There is no fee, no tier, no waiting list.

## What we look for

We are not just looking for winners. We are looking for tipsters who:

- **Show their work.** A line of reasoning behind each pick, even if
  it's brief.
- **Stick to a specialty.** "Premier League corners markets" beats
  "everything" every time.
- **Are comfortable with public losses.** Every tip you post stays
  visible — including the ones that don't come in.

We are explicitly *not* looking for people who want to use TipHub as a
funnel to a paid Telegram. We will spot it, and we will remove the
account.

## What your profile looks like

Once approved, you get a public profile at `/tipsters/your-name` with:

- Lifetime win rate, ROI, longest streak, total tip count
- Every tip you've ever posted, with its outcome
- Your specialty and short bio
- A "Hot" badge if you're in the top 3 by win rate that week

Anyone can follow your profile. Anyone can audit your numbers. That
audit-ability is the whole point.

## A word on expectations

A 60% lifetime win rate on 1.85-average odds is genuinely excellent and
rare. If you see somebody on the internet claiming 85%, they are either
cherry-picking, lying, or about to disappear. We'd rather have a
community of honest 55% tipsters than a leaderboard full of fake 80%
ones.

Welcome aboard. Apply when you're ready.
