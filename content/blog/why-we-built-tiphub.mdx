If you follow Serbian football tipping at all, you already know the pattern.
Somebody posts a winning slip on Instagram. The slip gets cropped, framed,
and sold as proof that a private Telegram channel is the way. You pay
€20-50 a month for predictions you can never audit. When picks lose, the
losses quietly vanish from the feed.

We are not interested in that game.

## What TipHub is

TipHub is a tipster community where **every single tip stays on public
record**. Wins, misses, ROI, longest streak — all of it sits on each
tipster's profile from the day they post. There is no hidden VIP tier
and no "premium pick" that bypasses the track record. If a tipster runs
cold for three weeks, you can see it. If they run hot, you can see that
too — and so can everyone else.

Three things are non-negotiable for us:

1. **Free forever.** No paywalls, no premium groups, no upsells.
2. **Best-odds first.** Every pick shows the offered price across five
   Serbian bookmakers, so you never bet 1.90 when somebody else has 2.05.
3. **Honest accounting.** Pending tips are pending. Lost tips stay lost.
   The leaderboard is the leaderboard.

## What it isn't

TipHub is not a betting operator. We do not take bets, hold balances, or
process payments. We are not affiliated with any of the bookmakers we
compare — Mozzart, Maxbet, Soccerbet, Meridian, Admiral — beyond
publishing their offered odds for transparency.

We are also not a tout service. Nobody at TipHub will DM you a "lock of
the day." If you see somebody using our brand to sell picks, it isn't us.

## What's next

We launched with World Cup 2026 group-stage coverage and a small founding
group of tipsters. Over the coming weeks we'll be opening tipster
applications more broadly, adding a public competition leaderboard, and
publishing weekly editorial breakdowns of the matches our community is
most active on.

If you want to be part of it — apply to become a tipster, or just start
following the ones whose numbers you trust. Either way, welcome.
